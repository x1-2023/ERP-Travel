# Training Video Scripts

## Video 1: Getting Started (5 min)

### Script:

"Chao mung den voi VietERP MRP - he thong quan ly san xuat toan dien.

Trong video nay, toi se huong dan ban:
1. Dang nhap vao he thong
2. Gioi thieu giao dien chinh
3. Cac module co ban

[Demo dang nhap]

Sau khi dang nhap, ban se thay Dashboard voi:
- KPI cards: hien thi so lieu quan trong
- Quick actions: tao nhanh cac records
- Alerts: canh bao can xu ly

[Demo navigate qua cac module]

Sidebar ben trai chua tat ca cac module:
- Parts: quan ly vat tu
- BOM: cau truc san pham
- Production: lenh san xuat
- Quality: kiem soat chat luong

Tren mobile, cac module nam o bottom navigation."

## Video 2: Parts Management (7 min)

### Script:

"Trong video nay, toi se huong dan quan ly Parts.

[Demo tao Part moi]

De tao Part:
1. Click nut Tao Part
2. Nhap Part Number - day la ma duy nhat
3. Nhap ten Part
4. Chon loai: Finished Good, Raw Material, Component...
5. Nhap gia cost
6. Click Luu

[Demo search va filter]

Ban co the tim kiem bang cach go vao o Search.
Filter theo category hoac status.

[Demo import Excel]

De import hang loat:
1. Click Import
2. Download template
3. Dien data vao file Excel
4. Upload va confirm"

## Video 3: Quality Module (10 min)

### Script:

"Quality Module la trai tim cua he thong QC.

[Demo NCR]

Khi phat hien loi:
1. Vao Quality > NCR
2. Tao NCR moi
3. Mo ta van de chi tiet
4. Danh gia severity
5. Assign cho nguoi xu ly

[Demo CAPA]

Tu NCR, tao CAPA:
1. Click Create CAPA
2. Phan tich Root Cause - tai sao loi xay ra
3. De xuat Corrective Action - sua loi ngay
4. De xuat Preventive Action - ngan loi tai dien
5. Verify effectiveness sau khi implement

[Demo Inspection Flow]

Quy trinh inspection:
1. Receiving: kiem tra hang nhap
2. In-Process: kiem tra trong san xuat
3. Final: kiem tra truoc xuat hang
4. Generate Certificate khi pass"

## Video 4: Discussions & Collaboration (5 min)

### Script:

"Discussions giup team giao tiep ngay tren he thong.

[Demo thao luan]

Tren moi record (Part, BOM, WO...), co tab Discussions.
Ban co the:
- Gui message
- @mention dong nghiep
- Dinh kem screenshot
- Link den entity khac

[Demo notification]

Khi duoc @mention, ban nhan notification ngay lap tuc.
Click vao notification de jump den discussion.

Real-time: messages xuat hien ngay khong can refresh."

## Video 5: BOM Management (8 min)

### Script:

"BOM - Bill of Materials la co so de tinh toan nguyen vat lieu.

[Demo tao BOM]

De tao BOM:
1. Vao menu BOM
2. Click Tao BOM moi
3. Chon Product
4. Them cac components:
   - Click Add Component
   - Chon Part
   - Nhap so luong
   - Nhap don vi
5. Luu BOM

[Demo xem cost]

BOM tu dong tinh:
- Material cost
- Labor cost (neu co)
- Total cost per unit

[Demo multi-level BOM]

BOM co the co nhieu cap:
- Level 0: Finished Good
- Level 1: Sub-assemblies
- Level 2: Components
- Level 3: Raw materials

Click 'Expand All' de xem full structure."

## Video 6: Work Orders & Production (8 min)

### Script:
